Hostels

Currently, there are five hostels spread over the Mandi town (one) and the Kamand Campus (four). Each hostel is managed by a Warden, ably assisted by an Assistant Warden, faculty members, a caretaker and a dedicated team of support staff and guards. There are two Girls's Hostels, Renuka in Mandi, and Chandrataal in Kamand. Five Boys's Hostels, Renuka in Mandi and Suvalsar ,Prashar, and Nako in Kamand.Students play an important role in the management of the hostels. They are represented by elected secretaries who help the Wardens in the day smooth running of the hostels. Messes catering to different hostels provide homely food. Common room is equipped with LCD TVs, table tennis facilities.

Kamand Campus

Nako
The Nako Hostel includes B6 and B7 block with a total capacity of 202. Facilities in the hostel include Wi-Fi connection in the rooms, LCD TV, washing machine etc. The warden, assistant warden and resident warden are ably assisted by a caretaker, support staff and guards. The elected student body gives their inputs for the smooth and proper functioning of the hostel on a daily basis.
Prashar
Prashar hostel is the first permanent hostel in the institute's main campus in Kamand.It constitutes the B1,B2 and G2 block.The building has been constructed deploying the latest technology of using pre-fabricated materials. The salient feature of this type of construction is that the rooms have excellent insulation, i.e., they remain cool during hot summers and warm during cold winters. Prashar hostel students have several facilities like several sports facilities (table-tennis, badminton, cricket, volleyball, etc.) within the premises, Wi-Fi internet connection, a 42 inch LCD television, top range music system, washing machines, RO water purifiers, etc.Prashar hostel is the first among all the hostels of IIT Mandi to move towards full computerization and office automation.
Suvalsar
Suvalsar hostel constitutes G3,G4 and B5 block with a total capacity of 224. B5 building has been constructed deploying the latest technology of using pre-fabricated materials whereas both the G3 and G4 blocks have been constructed deploying the latest technology of using Light Gauge Steel Framing (LGSF) materials. The salient feature of this type of construction is that the rooms have excellent insulation, i.e., they remain cool during hot summers and warm during cold winters.
Chandrataal
Chandra Taal is a Girls hostel with a touch of serenity is situated in the south campus of IIT Mandi. The hostel is a thermally insulated pre-fabricated building. Every room is furnished with cots, tables, chairs, almirahs, fans and tube lights. Facilities like 42 inch LED Television, washing machine, Hot and cold water, RO purifiers, Wi-Fi internet connection, and sports facility like badminton are available. Chandra Taal Annexe is extension of the Chandra Taal hostel on Kamand campus. Every room is furnished with cots, tables, chairs, almirahs, fans and tube lights. Facilities like 42 inch LED Television, washing machine, Hot and cold water, RO purifiers, Wi-Fi internet connection are also available.

Mandi Campus

Renuka Hall
Renuka is the mixed hostel for both boys and girls.It is located adjacent to the Administration Block, just above the Mandi Bus stand. Facilities in the hostel include an attached mess providing homely food, including South Indian items, a visitors' room, badminton court, Wi-Fi connection in the rooms, LCD TV, washing machine with helper to wash clothes, etc
