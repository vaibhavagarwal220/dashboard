Major Events


  • Exodia

    • Exodia is the annual techno-cultural fest of Indian Institute of Technology Mandi. It is a three-day-long event held every year. The first edition was held in 2012 and since then it has grown to be one of the largest student-run college fests of the Himalayas.It is one of the biggest events of the Himalayan region, and attracts a lot of crowd from all over North India. The fest also tries to promote the Himalayan region and showcase its culture and beauty by orchestrating some of its events for the same.

  • Rann-Neeti

    • Rann-Neeti is the sports fest of IIT Mandi , held in the odd semester and invites colleges from all over India to participate.This fest was conceptualised by Sports society in 2013 and the first edition was a great success.The second edition was held in 2014 and had doubled the participation.The fest witnesses sports like Cricket, Football, Hockey,Badminton,Table Tennis,Athletics,Basketball, Volleyball, Chess etc.

  • Aakarshan

    • Aakarshan is the inaugral cultural event of Cultural Society.It is mainly meant for freshers.One get to know the full range of activities of the various clubs under Cultural Society.

  • Utkarsh

    • Utkarsh is a relatively new event.It is an in-house technical event organized in the month of November by Technical council.Various technical clubs organize different types of competitions.

  • Aagaz

    • Aagaz is an inter-year sports tournament organized by the sports society in the month of March.

  • Exuberance

    • Exuberance is the main in-house cultural event of IIT Mandi and takes place in the even semester.This event has performances from all the cultural clubs.

  • Avishkar

    • Avishkar is the Intra college technical event organized by the techincal council in even semester.It includes many competitive events for various clubs.Avishkar also brings out a newsletter at regular intervals.

  • Lit-Fest

    • Lit-Fest is the Intra college literary event organized by the literary society in even semester.It includes events like Group Discussion , Quizzes, Just a Minute(JAM).2nd edition of Lit-Fest witnessed a poetry/spoken word show by "Delhi Poetry Slam".
